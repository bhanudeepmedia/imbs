Important Notice: IMBS programs follow international accreditation standards set by bodies in the US, UK, and India. These programs are not affiliated with UGC (University Grants Commission) and are therefore not eligible for Indian government employment or public sector roles that require UGC-recognised qualifications. IMBS credentials are designed for the private sector, global companies, multi-national corporations, and career advancement in internationally competitive industries. Students are advised to review program suitability against their individual career goals prior to enrollment.
